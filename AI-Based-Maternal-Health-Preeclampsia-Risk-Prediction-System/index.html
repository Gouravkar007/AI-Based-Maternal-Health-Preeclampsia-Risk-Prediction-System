What is Preeclampsia?

Preeclampsia is a pregnancy complication characterized by high blood pressure and signs of damage to other organ systems, most commonly the liver and kidneys. It typically begins after 20 weeks of pregnancy in women whose blood pressure had previously been normal.

If left untreated, preeclampsia can lead to serious complications for both mother and baby, including preterm birth and maternal death. Early detection is crucial for effective management and positive outcomes.

Affects 5-8% of all pregnancies worldwide

Two-Phase System

Phase 1: Maternal Health

The first phase assesses overall maternal health status using a comprehensive Random Forest model trained on the UCI Maternal Health dataset.

  • Classifies into Low, Mid, and High risk categories
  • Uses 6 key health indicators
  • 94% overall accuracy

Phase 2: Preeclampsia

The second phase specifically detects preeclampsia risk using a Logistic Regression model tuned with RandomizedSearchCV, trained exclusively on preeclampsia records with 13 clinical features. Other models (Random Forest, Decision Tree) were evaluated but showed signs of overfitting on this synthetic dataset, achieving near-perfect scores that would not generalise to real data.

  • 13 features: BP, BMI, hemoglobin, glucose, proteinuria, HIV, anemia & more
  • 88% accuracy · 89% cross-validation · 0.94 ROC-AUC
  • Gestational age ≥ 20 weeks enforced; BP & BMI range warnings built in

Why Logistic Regression?

Random Forest and Decision Tree achieved near-perfect (~100%) scores on this synthetic dataset, a strong signal of overfitting — memorising the data rather than learning generalisable patterns. Logistic Regression was chosen because its 88% accuracy, 89% cross-validation, and 0.94 ROC-AUC represent realistic, trustworthy performance that is expected to hold on unseen real-world data.

How It's Made

Our rigorous data processing pipeline ensures accurate, reliable predictions

Data Merging

Three CSV files (high, moderate, low burden) merged and filtered to preeclampsia records only. Dropped irrelevant columns.

Feature Engineering

Renamed columns, one-hot encoded Anemia_Status, label-encoded target, and enforced gestational age ≥ 20 weeks.

Scaling & Pipeline

StandardScaler on 8 numerical features inside a ColumnTransformer pipeline; categorical features passed through.

Hyperparameter Tuning

RandomizedSearchCV (30 iterations, 5-fold StratifiedKFold, ROC-AUC scoring) over C, penalty, solver, and class_weight.
